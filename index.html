The Avengers

The Avengers, is a 2012 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name.
In the film, Nick Fury and the spy agency S.H.I.E.L.D. recruit Tony Stark, Steve Rogers, Bruce Banner, Thor, Natasha Romanoff, and Clint Barton to form a team capable of stopping Thor's brother Loki from subjugating Earth.

Release date 2012

Director Joss Whedon

Cast Robert Downey Jr, Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson

Language English

IMBD rating-8.0★

Avengers Age Of Ultron

it is the sequel to The Avengers (2012) and the 11th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).
In the film, the Avengers fight Ultron, an artificial intelligence accidentally created by Tony Stark (Downey) and Bruce Banner (Ruffalo).
Ultron has the goal of causing human extinction in order to save the Earth.

Director Joss Whedon

Avengers:Infinity war
